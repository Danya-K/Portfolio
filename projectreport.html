Project Report

After me and my group conducted Surveys, Interviews, made two Personas and two User Scenarios each we were required to create a Project Report Document where we would state all of the findings that we made for the client and creating the website. Inside of the document included each of the Interviews we made and the key phrase from the Interviewee, we have Survey findings, here we also created two main Personas and User Scenarios based on the Personas each of us made. We also have all of the sources along with images stated at the bottom for comfortability. Except what I just mentioned we also have summary section under each task that we made. We also putted here the conclusion at the bottom of the document and the big summary at the top for the convinience of anyone who is going to read that document.
This document relates to the Communiction Outcome because in one document we gathered all of the main inforamtion each of us found and now anyone including teachers, client and us can read it.
