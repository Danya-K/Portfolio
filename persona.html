Personas/User Scenarios

After I conducted my Interview and we spread the survey among all of the people we wanted we begin to create our own personas and User Scenarios. Each of us had to create two Personas and User Scenarios based on the Interviews we made and Survey. From my Interview I've created 1 Persona as well as User Scenrio. In my first Persona I described the Person like that: He is a 20 year old Student in Fontys. He is Bulgarin and is working at Albert Heijn right now, in his free time he likes to hang out with friends and play games. When he buys jewellery he prefers ones that are cheap and can represent him as a Person. He prefers to go in real life shops rather than an online one. Based on that I've created the User Story for him where he wants to buy a jewellery he goes to the real shop, chooses the one he likes and buys it. The second Persona is based on the information that I didn't took from my Interview and I also took data from Survey. My second Persona is a 19 year old Student in Fontys, she works at Jumbo right now and in her free time she likes to hang out with friends and programm websites. She doesn't mind using websites for jewelry shopping but she mostly uses it for checking for new products or sales. Based on that Persona I made a User Storie with her buying the jewellery. In general, she goes to the inline shop to look for something interesting, if she finds something she buys it or goes to the shop to try it out and then buy.
This assignment relates to the Research Learning Outcome because with creating this Personas and User Scenarios I gained familliarity with the user and acquired new insights into a given situation.
