User Story/Task Analysis

After we did our Research with Testing and Survey we created a visual scenario of how our user would go to our website and buy the jewelery. After that we made task Analysis of the User which is basically a representation of how the User behaves and what tasks he acomplishes in order to buy a jewelery from our website.
Those two tasks relates to the Research Learning Outcome because it helps to understand who our main Target is and how they become it.
