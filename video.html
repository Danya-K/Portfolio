Video Documentary

Second most important assignment regarding Media Design we had was to make a video of 5 minutes on any topic and in any genre we like. each member of our team created his own scenario from which we later on choose one and changed it for filming. This was the first video, but we also made second one because in Canvas there was an asssignment for two. For the second one we decided to make a podcast where we talk about our first experiences in the Netherlands. It was my idea that we do podcast without any predefined answers so it feels more atmospheric. In both of the videos I took role as one of the main actors. In the first one I am playing role of a leader of the team so I appear the most on the screen and in the second one I am leading the conversation with questions while also answering on them.
The video assignment was second most important assignment in media design and that's why I am putting it here.
