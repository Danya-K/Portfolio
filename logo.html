Logo

One of the most important tasks that we are going to have during our studies are creating Logo that represents us. Here you can see the Logo I made for myself and the itterations of it. At first the idea was to make it look like a ring with my initials but the design was too bad for my taist so I started experimenting with it by adding or removing circles but keeping the shape circled because of the ring idea. You can see on the pictures something that looks like half of the moon or like a smile but the one in the center is the one that I choose because it was interesting looking and reminded of three things which is: eye with my initials as pupil, two half moons surrounding my initials and the ring with my initials inside of it. The feedback for the Logo was good, the teacher really liked the design and the backstory for it.
This task relates to this Learning Outcome because it shows me as a media designer and as a professional identity with my own creative ideas and look on things. It also relates to the Learning Outcome of Design because I apply industry standard tool called Adobe Illustrator.
