Planning

Our Gantt Chart provides a holistic, bird's-eye view of the entire project. It serves as a roadmap, illustrating major milestones, deadlines, and dependencies. By collaboratively engaging with the group, we've outlined critical tasks and allocated responsibilities, ensuring a shared understanding of the project's structure and overarching goals. For more granular, day-to-day task management, we utilize Trello. This platform allows us to break down the tasks identified in the Gantt Chart into smaller, manageable units. Each task becomes a Trello card, and we populate the board with these cards to create a dynamic workflow. Trello's flexibility is a key asset. We can easily adapt our task list based on changing priorities, unforeseen challenges, or new insights. The ability to assign tasks to specific team members, set due dates, and employ labels or categories for organization ensures that everyone has a clear understanding of their responsibilities. The synergy between the Gantt Chart and Trello Board enhances our project management capabilities. While the Gantt Chart provides a high-level overview and a structured plan, Trello excels in day-to-day execution and adaptability. This integrated approach facilitates seamless communication within the team, fostering collaboration and transparency. Regular updates and adjustments can be made in real-time, ensuring that our project stays on track and adapts to the evolving demands of our work.
This two activities that we are keeping eye on helps our group stay informed of what we do and are planning to do and that's why it belongs to the Communication Learning Outcome.
