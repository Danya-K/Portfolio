Competitive Analysis
Passion Project

The final step to conduct my Research was to create a Competitive Analysis. This was neccessary in order for me to understand how the sphere of Stationery web shops works, what is the standard and what features or design techniques do they use. For that I found three staionery stores on the web, which are: MUSTHAVESTORE.nl, Vanbeekart.nl, and Pipoos.com.
