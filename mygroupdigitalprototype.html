My Version of Group Prototype

In order to create a good design for the group project we agreed with the group that each of us will make his own design which we will later on combine into one. In order to create this design I took some ideas from other jewelery websites, I also did that to make my design consistent and in accordance with market standards. For the color scheme I used more soft and grey colors provided by our client both because they looked fitting and becuase that's what our client wanted. I created Low/Mid Fidelity Prototypes for that which you can see on the pictures, the reason I did the both in Figma is because it's much easier for me to look and understand how the website will look on the screen. For the About Client page the client asked that there should be a Gallery of his best products, I've implamanted that, I also had an idea in mind of a beatiful video showing his workplace which would attract customers and can be used as a commercial, so I found an AI tool called "InVideo" where you can make a video by describing what you want to see with text. After I made that video I've putted it in the header of the About Client Page, it is playable when you start demonstration so you can see it yourself.
This task relates to the Design Learning Outcome because it has layout and look and feel of media products. It is also relates to the Interactive Media Learning Outcome because it is a mid fidelity prototype that is clear to the User.
