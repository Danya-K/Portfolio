Survey

In order to create the website for the client we had to create Survey. We've created the Survey using DOT framework mehods by answering three main questions: What do we research, Why do we research that and How we research that. Answering the "What" question we made the survey to research what kind of experience users had with the jewellery websites if they had any and what are their priorities on the website. "Why" question is that we would use this gathered inforamtion to create the best possible web-site that will include as much useful functions and pleasing design as possible. And the last question "How" we answered by making the questions like "What was the website/websites that you visit to search for Jewellery?", or "What do you like about that website?" which would help us finding simmilar jewellery website the users like and use it for inspiration. Some of the questions I wrote for the Survey: "How often do you look at the jewellery website?" to help us build the Personas, "What types of the jewellery do you wear most frequently?" to help us prioritize what should in which order on our website and "What you do like and don't bout the website you visited?" to let us know what is best to put or to not put on the our website. I also spread the Survey among the two groups of 4 and 5 people making it in total 9 responses out of 32 we got.
This Survey that we made belongs to the Learning Outcome "Research" because I used Exploratory research and Methods provided in the Outcomes asking questions What Why and How and answering them with the questions in the Survey. And I also used Curious and Critical Attitude when I was spreading the Survey among the students.
