Photo Portfolio

Photo Portfolio is one of the main assignments for the whole Semester 2 of Media. In the bouklet there were quite a lot of different assignments requiring to use different settings of camera at different positions and places in order to complete the tasks correctly. After taking there also should have been created a portfolio for the photos where I would have to put the pictures, description of them and most importantly, what were the settings of the camera when the picture was taken as well as why exactly did you take that exact photo and why did you use this specific settings for taking that picture. I did all of the pictures from the Tasks and asked feedback from the teacher for that and it was positive in all of the parts except some photos that I had to change and then put them back into the Portfolio.
This assignment relates to the Design Learning Outcome because I was using Photoshop to style the pictures with White Balance and Contrast.
